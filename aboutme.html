ABOUT ME

Photographer/Videographer/Elementary School Teacher... COFFEE LOVER

Hi, my name is Ashley Paige and welcome to my world!

My love of photography all started when one vacant summer, I decided to pick up something that I have always been intrigued with and just started snapping away...

On my journey, I have had the opportunity to do various shoots for fashion, shoots for music professionals and many important clients, which has been a wonderful learning experience for me.
However, the moment I truly realized I could change the world was when I had my first maturity shoot and saw that my lens was capturing the true meaning of love. Yes, there’s editing and technical stuff, but that first picture you take and capture the love of a mother and her first child in a way they weren't ready for was just so rewarding. From here on, I realized my mission was that I wanted to just continue capturing true love behind every picture…

Whether it is the “I do’s” at weddings, sneakers, fashion, tall buildings, music, or videoshoots... As soon as I looked through that lens, I immediately see how I could change the world.
