ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Ashley Paige, Welcome to my portfolio website!

By profession, I teach elementary special Ed. But my heart resides in photography. It started when one vacant summer, I decided to pick up something that I have always been intrigued with, took my first shot and just couldn't stop snapping away.

It’s funny because one minute I was sitting on the couch trying to figure out what to do with myself, and next thing I know I’m at the biggest parties, biggest events, with big names as my clients. Videography started because I have always had a love for music.
I have had the opportunity to do various photoshoots for fashion and many important clients, which has been a wonderful learning experience for me. The moment I truly realized I could change the world was when I had my first maturity shoot and saw that my lens was capturing the love behind each picture.

Yes, there’s editing and technical stuff, but that first picture you take and capture the love of a mother and her first child in a way they weren't ready for was just so rewarding. From here on, I wanted to just continue capturing true love behind every picture… whether it was the “I dos” at weddings, shoes, or landscape. As soon as I looked through that lens, I saw how I could change the world.
